And everything else that says “Samuel Edward Konkin III” that you don’t see on our sites as well as titles we have already scanned if you can produce better quality. That includes many New Libertarian and New Libertarian Notes issues if you have access to the original and kopubco.com lists only photocopy.

Try to do scans in PNG format, with resolution of 600 DPI and full color (not just black and white). But we’ll probably accept anything you can do.

Some of the articles can be found and scanned in libraries, so even if you don’t own anything we need, you still can help. Please search on worldcat and see if you can visit a local library that have some of the titles above.

Feel free to email us if you not sure how to search, not sure if we need something you have, or not sure about anything. We don’t bite. matimatik@gmail.com
